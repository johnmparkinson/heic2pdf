How to Convert HEIC to PDF on Windows

  • Open the HEIC file in Preview.
  • Go to File > Export as PDF.
  • Select a location and click Save.

How to Convert HEIC to PDF on Mac

  • Open the Photos app and select the HEIC file.
  • Tap the Share button.
  • Select Print, then use a pinch-out gesture on the preview to open the PDF view.
  • Tap the Share button again to save or send the PDF.

How to Convert HEIC to PDF on iPhone

  • Open the HEIC file in Google Photos or Gallery.
  • Tap the Share button.
  • Select Print or Save as PDF.
  • Choose a save location and tap Save.

How to Convert HEIC to PDF on Android

  • Visit a site like CloudConvert or iLovePDF.
  • Upload your HEIC file.
  • Click Convert and download your PDF.
